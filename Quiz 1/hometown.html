I was born and raised in the bustling and busy city of Jakarta. Well . . . mostly, although I was born in Jakarta, my parent’s hometown is in Madura. While Jakarta has everything you will ever need with its abundant malls and amusement parks, getting around in Jakarta isn’t the best with traffic jams on basically everywhere you go. The glimmering lights in the city often felt contrasting to the glimmering stars in the quiet village, both spices things up and makes my live more wonderful.
